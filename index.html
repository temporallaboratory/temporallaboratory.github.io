- Musicbox

Musicbox is a simple device, not expensive to produce, that triggers an event and plays back music recorded on an SD card. The device was developed for use in parks and their revitalization.

The following are the components used to build the device:

  • Attiny 13A - Microcontroller
  • DY-SV17F - Intelligent Voice Module
  • JSN SR-04T - Waterproof Ultrasonic Sensor
  • TDA 2030A - Low-frequency amplifier

Also the manufacturing process of the device later led to a paper published in a Q3 level scientific journal:
